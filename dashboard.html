Government Spending In Kentucky During the COVID-19 Pandemic

This dashboard holds the visualizations for my data analysis on government spending during the pandemic in the state of Kentucky.

The most interesting pattern I found from my visualizations is that in contradiction to my original hypothesis population does not have as strong as an effect on grants recieved. In the bar graph depicting total grants recieved per county, Franklin county far outweighs all others. However, in the bar graph depicting population for each county, Franklin county has one of the lowest totals. I plan to dig deeper into this pattern during my EDA 3.
